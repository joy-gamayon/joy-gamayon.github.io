ABOUT ME AND WHY I CHOSE DEV ACADEMY





Hello Karaka fam, my name is Joy and I resided in the coal town of Huntly.
Lately, I've been contemplating the intersection of authenticity and societal expectations in shaping one's identity.
I believe that staying true to oneself while navigating societal norms and expectations can be a challenging yet rewarding journey.
I've chosen to attend Dev Academy because of my partner. He's in IT field and he is also interested in learning Web development,
but at the moment he's just focusing at his new job.
I know it would be great to study and to go through this journey together.
He was the one who introduced me to this Academy and encouraged me to start my web development journey.
I have been interested in this field due to its flexibilty to work remotely like my partner right now.
I am passionate about technology and its potential to drive positive change in the world. I see Dev Academy as an opportunity to
deepen my skills in software development, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and contribute to innovative projects that align with my values.
I'm excited to immerse myself in a supportive learning environment where I can grow both personally and professionally while making meaningful contributions to the tech community.
