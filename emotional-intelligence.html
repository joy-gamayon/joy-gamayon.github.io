What is Emotional Intelligence?

Based on what I have read and from research sources, Emotional intelligence (EI) or we can also called as Emotional quotient (EQ), is about having
the ability to understand, recognize and manage our own emotions, as well as the emotions of other people. Possessing such skills set could nagivate
our social interactions effectively, handle stressful situations, can communicate with empathy and making decisions through emotional cues.
There are key components of emotional intellgence which are important skills set to possess, as it navigates our learning journey at Dev Academy.
Hence, it can help us become more successful in Web Development industry.
These skills set are having the ability to become aware of ourselves like recognizing and understanding our own feelings and thoughts, strengths, weakness, motivations and our values.
To be able to manage and control our own emotions, impulse, and our reaction towards feelings and certain problems or situations, which are challenging for us.
To become aware and mindful to those people surround us, by being empathetic and undestanding their emotions, perspective in life and needs.
Thus, emotional intelligence plays a crucial role in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, leadership, teamwork, and overall well-being.
I believe people with high emotional intelligence would normally possess great leadership capabilities, internpersonal relationships and resilience when faced obstacles.
Additionally, they can effectively adapt to rapid changes within the environment and can handle stress more constructively.




How is EQ different to IQ?


For my understanding, Emotional Intelligence (EQ) and Intelligence Quotient (IQ) are both measures of human abilities, but they differ in their focus and what they measure.
Regarding Intelligence Quotient(IQ), this (IQ) mainly measures and focuses on the cognitive abilities of a person through problem-solving, mathematical ability,
logical reasoning, and verbal comprehension. While emotional intelligence (IQ) focuses on the ability of oneself and others by being able to recognize, understand,
manage, and express emotions effectively, as well as the ability to navigate social interactions and relationships. In addtion, we merely based IQ for
intellgence tests in assessing our cognitive abilities through tasks such as memory tests, puzzles, pattern recognition etc. On the other hand, EQ is more enclose
for possessing self-awareness, self-regulation, social awareness and relationship management. Which focuses on emotional skills rather than cognitive abilities of a person.
Therefore, IQ focuses on cognitive side of a person's abilities, while EQ focuses on emotional skills. Both are important for overall success and well-being,
but they represent different aspects of human intelligence and abilities.



Why is EQ important?


In terms of learning, posessing Emotional Intelligence (EQ) would be a helpful tool in handling our stress, worries,
and improving our relationship with peers. As we would be able to recognize and regulate our emotions.
Hence, stress can be manageable and we can remain resilient in challenging situations.
We also have better interpersonal relationships, as we become more empathetic and understanding towards people, and able to control or manage conflicts.
Thus, this leads to stronger connections with colleagues, friends, and family members.
In addition, a higher chance of becoming successful to any career paths we take, as possing EQ is increasingly valued in the workforce.
This is because people with high EQ can easily adapt to changing environments, communicate effectively, and
can work well in teams. Which is the most essential assets to have in every company in order to ultimately contribute to organizational success.
Overall, emotional intelligence is essential for navigating the complexities of human interactions, fostering positive relationships,
and achieving personal and professional success. It's a skill that can be developed and improved over time,
making it a valuable asset for individuals in all areas of life.
