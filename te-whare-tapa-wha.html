Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

To improve my physical wellbeing, I often go for a body massage 2-3 times a month.
Taking a hot bath at home every night time before I go to bed.
I work out 2-3 times a week during night time.
This is for my body to recover from muscle pain, stress and overall wellbeing of my physical health.







Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

I normally takecare of my mental health the same way as how I takecare of my physical health. I go for massage and work out to release some stress.
I always listen to music such as hillsongs (worships) before going to bed.
Taking a break from work and go to the beach and catching up with some friends once a week.
Having an alone time with myself, eating my favourite food and watching my favourite movies or dramas every day off.







Taha Wairua (spiritual wellbeing)

To improve my spritual health, I normally go to Church every Sundays to connect with God.
I pray and listen to some worship or calming songs every night before going to sleep.
This helps me spiritually connected with my body and mind, giving me a sense of calmness and peace of mind.
Also, spending time with nature every once a week and having an alone time with myself to reflect and to meditate everyday.







Taha Wh훮nau (family wellbeing)

Every once a week, I spend time with my family and friends. I go to Church with my family every Sunday.
I always have a get together with my relatives and other family members every weekend. Like having a bbq party and going to the beach or any family activities.
I always attend my friends special ocassions and always try to make time for them, through video calls or chats and catching up with them.
Offering support and help towards my community, family and friends. This interaction improves my relationship towards them and build stronger connections.







Whenua (land)

Our house itself has a lot of plants. We love to have plants inside our home because it gives positive atmosphere.
It brings good luck and good health to our family and to our home.
I like to spend time with nature every now and then by going to some parks, sitting or lying on a grass while listening to music or the sound of trees or birds and watching the sky.
As well as, going to the beach and lying on the sand while listening to the waves. I normally connect with nature when I need
to be away from stressful environment, and needing some time alone to reconnect with myself and to my body and soul.
